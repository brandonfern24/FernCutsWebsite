ABOUT

I’m a 23-year-old barber with 5 years of experience, dedicated to delivering clean, sharp cuts at an affordable price. My goal is to make every client look their best without breaking the bank — my services are as low as $10-$30. Book with me and leave feeling confident every time.

I specialize in fades, trims, line-ups, beard grooming, and shape-ups. Whether you need a crisp fade, a fresh edge, or a clean beard finish, I focus on precision and detail to give you the exact look you want.
